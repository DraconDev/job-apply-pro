Job Apply Pro Help

Getting Started

Job Apply Pro helps you automate your job application process on LinkedIn. Here's how to get started:

  1. 1. Click the extension icon to open the popup
  2. 2. Fill out your application information once using the "Edit Form Information" button
  3. 3. Navigate to LinkedIn Jobs
  4. 4. Start applying to jobs with one click!

Features

Form Auto-Fill

Save your common application information and let the extension fill it out for you.

Application History

Keep track of all your applications in one place. View when and where you applied.

One-Click Apply

Apply to LinkedIn Easy Apply jobs with a single click.

Tips for Success

  • • Keep your form information up to date
  • • Review each job posting before applying
  • • Check your application history to avoid duplicate applications
  • • Make sure your LinkedIn profile is complete and up to date
